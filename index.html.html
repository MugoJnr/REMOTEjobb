Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is KaziRemote legit? ▼
Absolutely. We've been operating since 2022 and have placed over 6,200 Kenyans in remote roles. We're registered with the Business Registration Service and partnered with verified employers worldwide.
Why do I need to pay to find a job? ▼
Your subscription covers our AI matching technology, employer vetting, profile optimization, and dedicated support. Think of it like investing KES 999 to potentially earn KES 50,000+ monthly. We vet every employer so you don't waste time on scams.
How do I pay via M-Pesa? ▼
Go to M-Pesa → Lipa na M-Pesa → Paybill → Business No: 247365 → Account: Your phone number. You'll receive instant confirmation via SMS and email. Airtel Money and card payments also accepted.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes! No contracts, no hidden fees. Cancel from your dashboard or text STOP to 22456. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
Do I need a degree or experience? ▼
Most of our jobs don't require a degree — over 60% are skills-based. Data entry, virtual assistance, customer support, transcription, and social media management are all available for beginners. We also have training resources to upskill.
What's the "5 interviews guarantee" on Premium? ▼
If you're on our Premium plan and don't receive at least 5 interview invitations within 30 days, we'll refund your subscription in full. No questions asked. We're that confident in our matching system.
